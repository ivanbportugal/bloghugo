Discovery

October 22, 2015

Well it looks like I keep starting new blogs over and over again but really I’m just playing around with what I think is neat. Since I love web technologies, fiddling around with Ghost, Medium, and other “cutting edge” blog platforms is really just a matter of self-discovery. I think I’ve settled on HUGO for the long term but we will see if someone comes up with something truly revolutionary in the future that will force me to reconsider.

Not that it’s even a big deal anyways since the content of this site/blog is what actually matters…
